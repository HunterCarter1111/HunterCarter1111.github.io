About this template

This is a compact, single-file HTML template meant to get you started. It's intentionally lightweight so you can host it directly on GitHub Pages. Customize the text, swap images, or add maps and regulations links for your state.

Hunting Checklist

Essentials

  • Valid hunting license & tags
  • Firearm / bow (in legal transport) + cleaning kit
  • Ammunition / arrows
  • Binoculars & rangefinder

Clothing & Camp

  • Layered clothing & rain gear
  • Orange safety vest / hat (if required)
  • First-aid kit, knife, multi-tool
  • Water, snacks, headlamp

Click Download Checklist to save a simple text file you can print or share.

Safety & Ethics

  • Follow all local laws, seasons, and bag limits.
  • Identify your target beyond doubt before shooting.
  • Wear required blaze orange or other visibility where mandated.
  • Practice humane, quick retrieval and field care.
  • Respect private property and ask permission before entering.
